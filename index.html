One Fast Can Change Your Life

The one day to reset your life — one day given to God, a rest given to the body.

There is one day, kept even once, that can quietly reset a life: the eleventh day of the moon, when you set the plate down, lighten the body, and turn the heart toward God.

The oldest scriptures call it the king of all vows. It rests and renews the body, quiets the restless senses, lifts the weight of old karma, and opens a real, two-way line to the Divine.

It asks no temple, no fee, no change of faith — not even a perfect attempt, to begin. Keep your own God. Just give one day. Written never to be sold, only to be given. Read it, or simply listen.
